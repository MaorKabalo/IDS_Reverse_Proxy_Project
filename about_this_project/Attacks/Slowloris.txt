Request Parsing and Buffering:
Input: Client requests.
Output: Parsed and buffered requests.
Description: Parse incoming requests to understand their content and intent. Buffering can help absorb Slowloris attacks by holding connections open until they are completed.


Connection Pooling and Management:
Input: Incoming connections.
Output: Managed and controlled connections.
Description: Maintain a pool of connections to handle client requests efficiently. This can prevent resource exhaustion attacks like Slowloris.
