Rate Limiting:
Input: Incoming requests.
Output: Throttled or denied requests.
Description: Implement rate limiting to restrict the number of requests a single client can make within a specific time frame. This can mitigate DDoS attacks by limiting their effectiveness.


DDoS Mitigation:
Input: Incoming traffic patterns.
Output: Traffic analysis and filtering.
Description: Employ traffic analysis algorithms to detect and filter out malicious traffic during a DDoS attack. This can involve identifying patterns, traffic anomalies, or excessive connection attempts.
